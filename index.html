About

I am a scientist working at the intersection of climate impacts, geomorphology, and data science. My research applies methods like geospatial analysis, machine learning, and causal inference to understand landscape dynamics and improve climate resilience.

Research

I aim to develop projects among three interrelated domains:

  • Climate Impacts: quantifying the effects of climate variability and water scarcity on land use, food systems, and sustainable agriculture.
  • Cascading Hazards: characterizing the cascading consequences of hydrological intensification on energy, infrastructure, and biodiversity.
  • Resilience Metrics: developing spatial indicators to inform pre-disaster planning.
