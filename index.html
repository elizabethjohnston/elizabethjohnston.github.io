About

Climate change intensifies cascading hazards among landscapes, endangering both societies and ecosystems. To address these environmental challenges, I apply geospatial analysis and remotely sensed data to understand the dynamics of Earthâ€™s systems under stress and change. Research

I ultimately aim to develop projects among three interrelated domains:

  • Climate impacts: quantifying the effects of climate variability on landscape evolution.
  • Cascading risks: characterizing the cascading consequences of hydrological intensification on natural resources and infrastructure.
  • Resilience metrics: developing spatial indicators to inform disaster response.
