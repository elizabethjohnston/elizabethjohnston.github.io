Research

To address these challenges, I develop projects among three interrelated domains:

  • Climate impacts: Quantifying the effects of climate variability on ecosystem-geomorphology feedbacks.
  • Decision support: Designing spatiotemporal indicators to inform disaster response and anticipatory adaptation.
  • Resilience metrics: Supporting land management and natural resources solutions to resolve the cascading consequences of hydrological intensification.
